\section{Observations}
Note that $f_k(n)$ is exactly equal to the number of $k$-ary partitions of $n$.  Looking at OEIS / empirical data, we find that
\[
 f_k(n) = f_k(n-1) + f_k \left (\left \lfloor \frac{n}{k} \right \rfloor \right)
\]
